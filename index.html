Friends of the Ladera Ranch Library


Friends of the Ladera Ranch Library, founded in the fall of 2003, is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization staffed solely by volunteers.

About Us - Library Info - Be a Friend - Join the Friends

Our Mission

Our mission is to form an association of persons interested in books and libraries, to focus attention on library services, facilities, and needs, and to stimulate gifts of books, magazines, desirable collections, endowments, and bequests.

More Info

Library Info

The Ladera Ranch Branch of the Orange County Public Library is located upstairs in the Administration Building of the Ladera Ranch Elementary and Middle School. This is a unique collaboration between the Capistrano Unified School District and the Orange County Public Library. Our knowledgeable and friendly staff are eager to help you find the information or reading materials you want. Besides our own collection, we can help you access the collections of 32 other libraries in the OC Libraries System. Look for our Business/Careers section, our Parenting section and our Children's and Teens sections. Programs include story times for infants and preschool children.

Be a friend

All funds received by the Friends of the Library through memberships, special events and contributions are used exclusively for the many projects the Library undertakes that are not funded by the Orange County Public Library System, e.g. The Summer Reading Program.

We want to improve and expand upon what our Library offers our community. You can help by becoming a member of the Friends of the Library. Members receive via email Friends' financial and statistical info prior to board meetings, volunteer opportunity, and invitations to attend board meetings; every member has a vote.
